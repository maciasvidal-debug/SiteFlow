## 2025-03-05 - Consolidate RLS policies and optimize PostgreSQL queries
**Learning:** Resolving 'Auth RLS Initialization Plan' and 'Multiple Permissive Policies' is crucial in Supabase. Wrapping `auth.uid()` calls with a subquery `(SELECT auth.uid())` allows the Postgres query planner to cache the call, preventing it from being needlessly evaluated on each row. Furthermore, merging multiple policies per table/action into single logic flows using `OR` removes redundant execution plans for overlapping roles. Added covering indexes on missing foreign keys to prevent sequential scans during cross-table queries.
**Action:** Replaced direct `auth.uid()` and `auth.jwt()` calls in RLS policies with subqueries. Combined overlapping permissive policies into singular optimized `ALL`, `SELECT`, `INSERT`, `UPDATE`, and `DELETE` policies on `activities`, `categories`, `protocols`, `profiles`, `audit_queries`, and `time_entries` tables. Added missing covering indexes.
